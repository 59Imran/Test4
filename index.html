Explore Our Destinations

Discover the most enchanting places for your perfect winter escape, each offering unique experiences and breathtaking views.

Srinagar

Srinagar, the serene summer capital, is famous for its picturesque Dal Lake. Enjoy relaxing houseboat stays and explore the vibrant Mughal Gardens.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg, the Meadow of Flowers, offers pristine ski slopes and the world's highest gondola. It's a paradise for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike.

Pahalgam

Pahalgam, the Valley of Shepherds, is renowned for its breathtaking valleys like Betaab and Aru. It's an ideal destination for serene nature walks and invigorating treks.

Sonamarg

Sonamarg, the Meadow of Gold, serves as a stunning gateway to Ladakh. Discover the majestic Thajiwas Glacier and enjoy the picturesque alpine scenery.

Yusmarg

Yusmarg, known as the Meadow of Jesus, is an unspoiled destination with serene landscapes. It's perfect for peaceful retreats, leisurely walks, and enjoyable horse riding experiences.

Doodhpathri

Doodhpathri, the Valley of Milk, is a stunning saucer-shaped valley. It boasts lush green meadows and a beautiful meandering river, offering a tranquil escape.
